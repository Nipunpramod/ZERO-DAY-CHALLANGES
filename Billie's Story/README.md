## 🧩 Challenge Description

Billie was a brilliant developer and he is the monster of the story , but brilliance often attracts the wrong kind of attention. After a sudden disappearance and a suspicious remote wipe of their workstation, all that remains is a fragmented forensic image.

The digital footprints tell a story of a struggle—not physical, but a battle of permissions and hidden directories. 

The investigation requires you to act as a forensic analyst. You must reconstruct the timeline, recover the deleted fragments, and uncover the secret Billie was trying to protect from the root of the system.

> *"The truth isn't lost; it's just waiting for the right forensic tool."*
